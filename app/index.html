What is STEMHacks?

STEMHacks is one of the very first high-school hackathons in Pennsylvania who’s mission it is to inspire the youth to program. In 24 hours 200+ students from the tri-state area will create a project from scratch that will be judged from some of the leaders in the tech industry for a chance to win amazing prizes. With unlimited food, drink, interesting demos and workshops, and of absolutely no cost, we can’t wait to see you there!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is usually a 24-48 hour programming competition in which participants create a project from scratch to win amazing prizes!

How much does it cost to attend?

Absolutely nothing! The entire event, food drink and all comes free of charge! However, we will not be reimbursing for travel costs.

Can I still sleep there?

Definitely! There will be rooms allocated specifically for those who wish to not stay up the full 24 hours.

What If I'm vegetarian/have an allergy?

Every meal will always have a vegetarian option to make sure you're covered. Also, no food will contain peanuts.

Can I work on the project beforehand?

Kind-of. You may plan and brainstorm the project beforehand, but all artwork/code/hardware must be created only during the event.

What if I don't know to code?

There will be free programming workshops you can attend, or you can learn how to program during those 24 hours through online tutorials!

What if I don't have a team?

You can find a team there, work by yourself, or ask on popular high school hacker facebook groups like HS Hackers

How safe will it be during the night?

There will be dedicated security guards patrolling the perimeter, and no student will be allowed to leave or enter once the competition starts for safety/fairness.
